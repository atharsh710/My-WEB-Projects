About Me

Atharsh Bodke

Hello Everyone! I am Atharsh Bodke, a 19 Year Old, who every so often loves to write Quatrains /Write-ups to engrave tons of thoughts on a piece of paper (or now-a-days online). Regarding my studies I am a Student who is fond of programming and computers and that’s the reason had this idea to create website.


The main motive to create this website was to get that experience as a Student and also know you all better through this medium. On November 5, 2019, I started posting my Poems/Quatrains/Write-ups on poetic windows by using Instagram as my sharing platform. I love the whole Experience from meeting new Poets on Instagram to learning many things from sharing my write-ups publicly.


Something else that I also want to share is that ‘I believe in words engraved should be more simple and humble towards the reader’, I mostly express words for not putting my vocabulary but to express what I feel and love to share it with you all.


My father often tells me about this proverb “तूच आहेस तुझ्या जीवनाचा शिल्पकार” which translate to “You are the architect of your life” is something which I always look forward to go all out and begin my day.


Long story short, I am grateful to each one of you for choosing to spend some of your valuable time on my website and stopping by to know and listen to me and I am always there to listen to you. Have a wonderful and pleasant day :)
